This privacy policy describes how Hexandcube ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit hexandcube.com, and/or any related services.

This policy applies to services hosted under the following domain names (unless otherwise specified): 
- `*.hexandcube.com`
- `*.floofware.net`
- `*.vulpy.link`

### 1. Data Controller

I (Hexandcube) am the data controller for the personal data collected through this website.
All data transmitted between your browser and this website is encrypted using SSL/TLS (HTTPS) encryption.

For any questions regarding your data, you can reach me at: hexandcube@hexandcube.com

### 2. What Data I Collect & Why

I only collect data that is necessary to keep the site functional and to communicate with users who reach out to me.

###### Contact Form 

If you use my contact form, I collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. 
I use this exclusively to reply to you. I do not use these emails for marketing or sell them to third parties.

Contact form submissions are processed by Netlify Forms, a service provided by Netlify, Inc. 
Netlify acts as a data processor on my behalf and handles data per their processing agreement.

###### Analytics (Umami)

I use a managed instance of Umami to see how many people visit my site. While Umami is privacy-focused,
it does process IP addresses to generate unique visitor stats. This data is used to understand site traffic and is not used to identify you personally.

###### Infrastructure

The hexandcube.com website and related services may use Cloudflare (a service provided by Cloudflare, Inc.) as a CDN and DNS proxy. Cloudflare processes your IP address and other connection data to route your requests to and from the origin server, and protect the site from malicious traffic. This data is not used for any other purpose and is not shared with third parties for marketing or advertising.

Your IP address is also processed by our hosting provider (Netlify, Inc) in order to provide the service and deliver the website content to you. This data is not used for marketing or sold to third parties.

Additionally, Your IP address may be processed by Backblaze B2 (a service provided by Backblaze, Inc.) for the purpose of delivering media content hosted on their infrastructure. This data is not used for marketing or sold to third parties.

### 5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Since I operate within the EU (Poland), you have the following rights under the GDPR:

- The right to access the data I have about you.
- The right to rectify (fix) any incorrect data.
- The right to erasure ("be forgotten") and have your contact form history deleted.
- The right to object to processing.
- The right to restrict processing.

Exercising your right to object or to restrict processing will generally be treated as a request to delete your data, unless you specify otherwise.

If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact me. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Polish data protection authority - The Office for Personal Data Protection (Urząd Ochrony Danych Osobowych - UODO, https://www.uodo.gov.pl/).
